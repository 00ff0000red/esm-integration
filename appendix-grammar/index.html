Appendix: Grammar Summary¶

Numbers¶

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llll} \mbox{(unsigned integers)} & {\mathit{uint}}_N &::=& 0 ~|~ 1 ~|~ \dots ~|~ 2^N{-}1 \\ \mbox{(signed integers)} & {\mathit{sint}}_N &::=& -2^{N-1} ~|~ \dots ~|~ {-}1 ~|~ 0 ~|~ 1 ~|~ \dots ~|~ 2^{N-1}{-}1 \\ ~ \\ \mbox{(floating-point numbers)} & {\mathit{float}}_N &::=& b^{N/8} \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

Types¶

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llll} \mbox{(value types)} & \mathit{valtype} &::=& \mathsf{i32} ~|~ \mathsf{i64} ~|~ \mathsf{f32} ~|~ \mathsf{f64} \\ \mbox{(result types)} & \mathit{resulttype} &::=& \mathit{valtype}^? \\ \mbox{(function types)} & \mathit{functype} &::=& \mathit{valtype}^\ast \to \mathit{resulttype} \\ ~ \\ \mbox{(table types)} & \mathit{tabletype} &::=& \mathit{elemtype}[\mathit{limits}] \\ \mbox{(memory types)} & \mathit{memtype} &::=& [\mathit{limits}] \\ \mbox{(limits)} & \mathit{limits} &::=& {\mathit{uint}_{32}}~{\mathit{uint}_{32}}^? \\ \mbox{(element types)} & \mathit{elemtype} &::=& \mathsf{anyfunc} \\ ~ \\ \mbox{(global types)} & \mathit{globaltype} &::=& \mathit{mutability}~\mathit{valtype} \\ \mbox{(mutability)} & \mathit{mutability} &::=& \mathsf{mut}^? \\ ~ \\ \mbox{(external types)} & \mathit{externtype} &::=& \mathsf{func}~\mathit{functype} ~|~ \mathsf{table}~\mathit{tabletype} ~|~ \\&&& \mathsf{memory}~\mathit{memtype} ~|~ \mathsf{global}~\mathit{globaltype} \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

Indices¶

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llll} \mbox{(type indices)} & \mathit{typeindex} &::=& {\mathit{uint}_{32}} \\ \mbox{(function indices)} & \mathit{funcindex} &::=& {\mathit{uint}_{32}} \\ \mbox{(table indices)} & \mathit{tableindex} &::=& {\mathit{uint}_{32}} \\ \mbox{(memory indices)} & \mathit{memindex} &::=& {\mathit{uint}_{32}} \\ \mbox{(global indices)} & \mathit{globalindex} &::=& {\mathit{uint}_{32}} \\ \mbox{(local indices)} & \mathit{localindex} &::=& {\mathit{uint}_{32}} \\ \mbox{(label indices)} & \mathit{labelindex} &::=& {\mathit{uint}_{32}} \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]
