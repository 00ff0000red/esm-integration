Iterations¶

Vectors¶

All vectors are encoded as sequences preceded by their length.

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{lll@{\qquad\qquad}l} \def\encodeinternal#1#2{\mathrm{encode}_{#2}(#1)}\encodeinternal{[x^n]}{t^\ast} &=& \def\encodeinternal#1#2{\mathrm{encode}_{#2}(#1)}\encodeinternal{x^n}{t^\ast} \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

Strings¶

  • \({\mathit{string}}\): strings are encoded as a vector of bytes:

    \[\begin{split}\begin{array}{lll@{\qquad\qquad}l} \def\encodeinternal#1#2{\mathrm{encode}_{#2}(#1)}\encodeinternal{s} &=& \mathrm{bytes}(z) \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]
