Instantiation¶

Given a store \(S\) and a module \(m\) is instantiated with a list of external values \(\href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-externval}{\mathit{externval}}^\ast\) supplying the required imports as follows.

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llll} \mathrm{alloctable}(S, \href{../syntax/modules.html#syntax-table}{\mathit{table}}) &=& S', \href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-tableaddr}{\mathit{tableaddr}} \\[1ex] \mbox{where:} \\[1ex] \href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-tableinst}{\mathit{tableinst}} &=& \{ \mathsf{elem}~(\epsilon)^{\href{../syntax/types.html#syntax-limits}{\mathit{limits}}.\mathsf{min}}, \mathsf{max}~\href{../syntax/types.html#syntax-limits}{\mathit{limits}}.\mathsf{max} \} & (\href{../syntax/modules.html#syntax-table}{\mathit{table}}.\mathsf{type} = \href{../syntax/types.html#syntax-limits}{\mathit{limits}}~\href{../syntax/types.html#syntax-elemtype}{\mathit{elemtype}}) \\ \href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-tableaddr}{\mathit{tableaddr}} &=& |S.\mathsf{tables}| \\ S' &=& S~\mbox{with}~\mathsf{tables} = S.\mathsf{tables}~\href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-tableinst}{\mathit{tableinst}} \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]
\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llll} \mathrm{allocmem}(S, \href{../syntax/modules.html#syntax-mem}{\mathit{mem}}) &=& S', \href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-memaddr}{\mathit{memaddr}} \\[1ex] \mbox{where:} \\[1ex] \href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-meminst}{\mathit{meminst}} &=& \{ \mathsf{data}~(\def\mathdef33#1{\mathrm{0x#1}}\mathdef33{00})^{\href{../syntax/types.html#syntax-limits}{\mathit{limits}}.\mathsf{min}}, \mathsf{max}~\href{../syntax/types.html#syntax-limits}{\mathit{limits}}.\mathsf{max} \} & (\href{../syntax/modules.html#syntax-mem}{\mathit{mem}}.\mathsf{type} = \href{../syntax/types.html#syntax-limits}{\mathit{limits}}) \\ \href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-memaddr}{\mathit{memaddr}} &=& |S.\mathsf{mems}| \\ S' &=& S~\mbox{with}~\mathsf{mems} = S.\mathsf{mems}~\href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-meminst}{\mathit{meminst}} \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]
\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llll} \mathrm{allocglobal}(S, \href{../syntax/modules.html#syntax-global}{\mathit{global}}) &=& S', \href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-globaladdr}{\mathit{globaladdr}} \\[1ex] \mbox{where:} \\[1ex] \href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-globalinst}{\mathit{globalinst}} &=& \{ \mathsf{value}~(t.\href{../syntax/instructions.html#syntax-instr-numeric}{\mathsf{const}}~0), \href{../syntax/types.html#syntax-mut}{\mathsf{mut}}~\href{../syntax/types.html#syntax-mut}{\mathit{mut}} \} & (\href{../syntax/modules.html#syntax-global}{\mathit{global}}.\mathsf{type} = \href{../syntax/types.html#syntax-mut}{\mathit{mut}}~t) \\ \href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-globaladdr}{\mathit{globaladdr}} &=& |S.\mathsf{globals}| \\ S' &=& S~\mbox{with}~\mathsf{globals} = S.\mathsf{globals}~\href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-globalinst}{\mathit{globalinst}} \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]
\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llll} \mathrm{alloc}X^\ast(S_0, X^n) &=& S_n, a^n \\[1ex] \mbox{where:} \\[1ex] (S_{i+1}, a &=& \mathrm{alloc}X(S_i, X))_i^n \end{array}\end{split}\]
\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llll} \mathrm{allocmodule}(S, \href{../syntax/modules.html#syntax-module}{\mathit{module}}, \href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-externval}{\mathit{externval}}_{\mathrm{im}}^\ast) &=& S', \href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-moduleinst}{\mathit{moduleinst}} \\[1ex] \mbox{where:} \mathsf{tables}~\href{../syntax/types.html#syntax-externtype}{\mathrm{tables}}(\href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-externval}{\mathit{externval}}_{\mathrm{im}}^\ast)~\href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-tableaddr}{\mathit{tableaddr}}^\ast, (\href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-moduleinst}{\mathit{moduleinst}}.\mathsf{funcs}[x])^\ast & (x^\ast = \href{../syntax/types.html#syntax-externtype}{\mathrm{funcs}}(\href{../syntax/modules.html#syntax-module}{\mathit{module}}.\mathsf{exports})) \\ \[\frac{ \href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-funcinst}{\mathit{funcinst}}.\mathsf{module}.\mathsf{types}[\href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-funcinst}{\mathit{funcinst}}.\mathsf{func}.\mathsf{type}] = \href{../syntax/types.html#syntax-functype}{\mathit{functype}} }{ S \vdash \mathsf{func}~\href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-funcinst}{\mathit{funcinst}} : \mathsf{func}~\href{../syntax/types.html#syntax-functype}{\mathit{functype}} }\]

\[\frac{ S.\mathsf{mems}[\href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-memaddr}{\mathit{memaddr}}] = \{ \mathsf{data}~b^{n\cdot64\,\mathrm{Ki}}, \mathsf{max}~m \} }{ S \vdash \mathsf{mem}~\href{../execution/conventions.html#syntax-tableaddr}{\mathit{tableaddr}} : \mathsf{mem}~\{ \mathsf{min}~n, \mathsf{max}~m \} }\]
