Auxiliary Operations¶

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{lll@{\qquad}l} \href{../execution/numerics.html#aux-bytes}{\mathrm{bytes}}_N(i) &=& \epsilon & (N = 0 \wedge i = 0) \\ \href{../execution/numerics.html#aux-bytes}{\mathrm{bytes}}_N(i) &=& b~\href{../execution/numerics.html#aux-bytes}{\mathrm{bytes}}_{N-8}(j) & (N \geq 8 \wedge i = 8\cdot j + b) \\ ~ \\ \href{../execution/numerics.html#aux-bytes}{\mathrm{bytes}}_{\mathsf{i}N}(i) &=& \href{../execution/numerics.html#aux-bytes}{\mathrm{bytes}}_N(i) \\ \href{../execution/numerics.html#aux-bytes}{\mathrm{bytes}}_{\mathsf{f}N}(b^N) &=& \mathrm{reverse}(b^N) \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]
\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{lll@{\qquad}l} \href{../execution/numerics.html#aux-signed}{\mathrm{signed}}_N(i) &=& i & (0 \leq i < 2^{N-1}) \\ \href{../execution/numerics.html#aux-signed}{\mathrm{signed}}_N(i) &=& i - 2^N & (2^{N-1} \leq i < 2^N) \\ \href{../execution/numerics.html#aux-signed}{\mathrm{signed}}_N(-i) &=& -i & (0 < i \geq 2^{N-1}) \\ ~ \\ \href{../execution/numerics.html#aux-extend}{\mathrm{extend}}_{\mathtt{U},N}(i) &=& i \\ \href{../execution/numerics.html#aux-extend}{\mathrm{extend}}_{\mathtt{S},N}(i) &=& \href{../execution/numerics.html#aux-signed}{\mathrm{signed}}_N(i) \\ \href{../execution/numerics.html#aux-wrap}{\mathrm{wrap}}_N(i) &=& i \mathbin{\mathrm{mod}} 2^N \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

Note

The index \(N\) of the \(\href{../execution/numerics.html#aux-extend}{\mathrm{extend}}\) function is the size extending from, where as the index of the \(\href{../execution/numerics.html#aux-wrap}{\mathrm{wrap}}\) function is the size wrapping to.
