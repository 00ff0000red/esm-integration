Result Types¶

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll@{\qquad\qquad}l} \def\mathdef2577#1{{}}\mathdef2577{result type} & \href{../text/types.html#text-resulttype}{\mathtt{resulttype}} &::=& (t{:}\href{../text/types.html#text-functype}{\mathtt{result}})^? &\Rightarrow& [t^?] \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

Note

In future versions of WebAssembly, this scheme may be extended to support multiple results or more general result types.

Abbreviations¶

Multiple anonymous parameters or results may be combined into a single declaration:

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll} \def\mathdef2577#1{{}}\mathdef2577{parameter} & \def\mathdef2641#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2641{(}~~\def\mathdef2642#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2642{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef2643#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2643{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef2644#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2644{(}~~\def\mathdef2645#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2645{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef2646#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2646{)})^\ast \\ \def\mathdef2577#1{{}}\mathdef2577{result} & \def\mathdef2647#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2647{(}~~\def\mathdef2648#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2648{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef2649#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2649{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef2650#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2650{(}~~\def\mathdef2651#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2651{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef2652#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2652{)})^\ast \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]
