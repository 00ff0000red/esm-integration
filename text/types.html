Result Types¶

Abbreviations¶

Multiple anonymous parameters or results may be combined into a single declaration:

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll} \def\mathdef2997#1{{}}\mathdef2997{parameter} & \def\mathdef3067#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3067{(}~~\def\mathdef3068#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3068{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef3069#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3069{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef3070#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3070{(}~~\def\mathdef3071#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3071{param}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef3072#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3072{)})^\ast \\ \def\mathdef2997#1{{}}\mathdef2997{result} & \def\mathdef3073#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3073{(}~~\def\mathdef3074#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3074{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}^\ast~~\def\mathdef3075#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3075{)} &\equiv& (\def\mathdef3076#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3076{(}~~\def\mathdef3077#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3077{result}~~\href{../text/types.html#text-valtype}{\mathtt{valtype}}~~\def\mathdef3078#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef3078{)})^\ast \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]
