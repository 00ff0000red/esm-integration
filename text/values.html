Values¶

The grammar produtions in this section define lexical syntax, hence no white space is allowed.

Integers¶

All integers can be written in either decimal or hexadecimal notation.

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll@{\qquad}l} \def\mathdef2141#1{{}}\mathdef2141{sign} & \href{../text/values.html#text-sign}{\mathtt{sign}} &::=& \epsilon \Rightarrow {+}1 ~|~ \def\mathdef2189#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2189{+} \Rightarrow {+}1 ~|~ \def\mathdef2190#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2190{-} \Rightarrow {-}1 \\ \def\mathdef2141#1{{}}\mathdef2141{decimal digit} & \href{../text/values.html#text-digit}{\mathtt{digit}} &::=& \def\mathdef2191#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2191{0} \Rightarrow 0 ~|~ \dots ~|~ \def\mathdef2192#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2192{9} \Rightarrow 9 \\ \def\mathdef2141#1{{}}\mathdef2141{hexadecimal digit} & \href{../text/values.html#text-hexdigit}{\mathtt{hexdigit}} &::=& d{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-digit}{\mathtt{digit}} \Rightarrow d \\ &&|& \def\mathdef2193#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2193{A} \Rightarrow 10 ~|~ \dots ~|~ \def\mathdef2194#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2194{F} \Rightarrow 15 \\ &&|& \def\mathdef2195#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2195{a} \Rightarrow 10 ~|~ \dots ~|~ \def\mathdef2196#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2196{f} \Rightarrow 15 \\ \def\mathdef2141#1{{}}\mathdef2141{decimal number} & \href{../text/values.html#text-num}{\mathtt{num}} &::=& d{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-digit}{\mathtt{digit}} &\Rightarrow& d \\ &&|& n{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-num}{\mathtt{num}}~~d{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-digit}{\mathtt{digit}} &\Rightarrow& 10\cdot n + d \\ \def\mathdef2141#1{{}}\mathdef2141{hexadecimal number} & \href{../text/values.html#text-hexnum}{\mathtt{hexnum}} &::=& \def\mathdef2197#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2197{0x}~~h{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-hexdigit}{\mathtt{hexdigit}} &\Rightarrow& h \\ &&|& n{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-hexnum}{\mathtt{hexnum}}~~h{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-hexdigit}{\mathtt{hexdigit}} &\Rightarrow& 16\cdot n + h \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

Integer literals are distinguished by size and signedness. Their value must lie within the range of the respective type.

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll@{\qquad}l} \def\mathdef2141#1{{}}\mathdef2141{unsigned integer} & \def\mathdef2198#1{{\mathtt{u}#1}}\mathdef2198{N} &::=& n{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-num}{\mathtt{num}} &\Rightarrow& n & (n < 2^N) \\ &&|& n{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-hexnum}{\mathtt{hexnum}} &\Rightarrow& n & (n < 2^N) \\ \def\mathdef2141#1{{}}\mathdef2141{signed integer} & \def\mathdef2199#1{{\mathtt{s}#1}}\mathdef2199{N} &::=& s{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-sign}{\mathtt{sign}}~~n{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-num}{\mathtt{num}} &\Rightarrow& s\cdot n & (-2^{N-1} \leq s\cdot n < 2^{N-1}) \\ &&|& s{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-sign}{\mathtt{sign}}~~n{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-hexnum}{\mathtt{hexnum}} &\Rightarrow& s\cdot n & (-2^{N-1} \leq s\cdot n < 2^{N-1}) \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

Uninterpreted integers can be written as either signed or unsigned, and are normalized to unsigned in the abstract syntax.

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll@{\qquad\qquad}l} \def\mathdef2141#1{{}}\mathdef2141{uninterpreted integers} & \def\mathdef2200#1{{\mathtt{i}#1}}\mathdef2200{N} &::=& n{:}\def\mathdef2201#1{{\mathtt{u}#1}}\mathdef2201{N} &\Rightarrow& n \\ &&|& i{:}\def\mathdef2202#1{{\mathtt{s}#1}}\mathdef2202{N} &\Rightarrow& n & (i = \signed(n)) \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

Floating-Point¶

Floating point values can be represented in either decimal or hexadecimal notation. The value of a literal must not lie outside the representable range of the corresponding IEEE 754 type (that is, it must not overflow to :math:`pm`infinity), but it may be rounded to the nearest representable value.

Note

Rounding can be prevented by using hexadecimal notation with no more significant bits than supported by the required type.

Floating-point values may also be written as constants for infinity or canonical NaN (not a number). Furthermore, arbitrary NaN values may be expressed by providing an explicit payload value.

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll@{\qquad\qquad}l} \def\mathdef2141#1{{}}\mathdef2141{decimal floating-point fraction} & \href{../text/values.html#text-frac}{\mathtt{frac}} &::=& \epsilon &\Rightarrow& 0 \\ &&|& d{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-digit}{\mathtt{digit}}~q{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-frac}{\mathtt{frac}} &\Rightarrow& (d+q)/10 \\ \def\mathdef2141#1{{}}\mathdef2141{hexadecimal floating-point fraction} & \href{../text/values.html#text-hexfrac}{\mathtt{hexfrac}} &::=& \epsilon &\Rightarrow& 0 \\ &&|& h{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-hexdigit}{\mathtt{hexdigit}}~q{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-hexfrac}{\mathtt{hexfrac}} &\Rightarrow& (h+q)/16 \\ \def\mathdef2141#1{{}}\mathdef2141{decimal floating-point number} & \href{../text/values.html#text-float}{\mathtt{float}} &::=& s{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-sign}{\mathtt{sign}}~p{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-num}{\mathtt{num}}~\def\mathdef2203#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2203{.}~q{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-frac}{\mathtt{frac}} &\Rightarrow& s\cdot(p+q) \\ &&|& s{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-sign}{\mathtt{sign}}~p{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-num}{\mathtt{num}}~(\def\mathdef2204#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2204{E}~|~\def\mathdef2205#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2205{e})~t{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-sign}{\mathtt{sign}}~e{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-num}{\mathtt{num}} &\Rightarrow& s\cdot p\cdot 10^{t\cdot e} \\ &&|& s{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-sign}{\mathtt{sign}}~p{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-num}{\mathtt{num}}~\def\mathdef2206#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2206{.}~q{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-frac}{\mathtt{frac}}~(\def\mathdef2207#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2207{E}~|~\def\mathdef2208#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2208{e})~t{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-sign}{\mathtt{sign}}~e{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-num}{\mathtt{num}} &\Rightarrow& s\cdot(p+q)\cdot 10^{t\cdot e} \\ \def\mathdef2141#1{{}}\mathdef2141{hexadecimal floating-point number} & \href{../text/values.html#text-hexfloat}{\mathtt{hexfloat}} &::=& s{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-sign}{\mathtt{sign}}~p{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-hexnum}{\mathtt{hexnum}}~\def\mathdef2209#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2209{.}~q{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-hexfrac}{\mathtt{hexfrac}} &\Rightarrow& s\cdot(p+q) \\ &&|& s{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-sign}{\mathtt{sign}}~p{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-hexnum}{\mathtt{hexnum}}~(\def\mathdef2210#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2210{P}~|~\def\mathdef2211#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2211{p})~t{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-sign}{\mathtt{sign}}~e{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-num}{\mathtt{num}} &\Rightarrow& s\cdot p\cdot 2^{t\cdot e} \\ &&|& s{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-sign}{\mathtt{sign}}~p{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-hexnum}{\mathtt{hexnum}}~\def\mathdef2212#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2212{.}~q{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-hexfrac}{\mathtt{hexfrac}}~(\def\mathdef2213#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2213{P}~|~\def\mathdef2214#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2214{p})~t{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-sign}{\mathtt{sign}}~e{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-num}{\mathtt{num}} &\Rightarrow& s\cdot(p+q)\cdot 2^{t\cdot e} \\ \def\mathdef2141#1{{}}\mathdef2141{floating-point value} & \def\mathdef2215#1{{\mathtt{f}#1}}\mathdef2215{N} &::=& z{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-float}{\mathtt{float}} &\Rightarrow& b^\ast & (\ieee_N(z) = b^\ast) \\ &&|& z{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-hexfloat}{\mathtt{hexfloat}} &\Rightarrow& b^\ast & (\ieee_N(z) = b^\ast) \\ &&|& s{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-sign}{\mathtt{sign}}~\def\mathdef2216#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2216{inf} &\Rightarrow& b^\ast & (\ieeeinf_N(s) = b^\ast) \\ &&|& s{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-sign}{\mathtt{sign}}~\def\mathdef2217#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2217{nan} &\Rightarrow& b^\ast & (\ieeenan_N(s, 0) = b^\ast) \\ &&|& s{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-sign}{\mathtt{sign}}~\def\mathdef2218#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2218{nan\!:}~n{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-hexnum}{\mathtt{hexnum}} &\Rightarrow& b^\ast & (\ieeenan_N(s, n) = b^\ast) \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

Vectors¶

Vectors are written as ordinary sequences, but with restricted length.

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll@{\qquad\qquad}l} \def\mathdef2141#1{{}}\mathdef2141{vector} & \href{../text/values.html#text-vec}{\mathtt{vec}}(\mathtt{A}) &::=& (x{:}\mathtt{A})^n &\Rightarrow& x^n & (n < 2^{32}) \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

Strings¶

Strings denote sequences of bytes that can represent both textual and binary data. They are enclosed in quotation marks and may contain any printable ASCII character other than quotation marks (\(\def\mathdef2219#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2219{"}\)) or backslash (\(\def\mathdef2220#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2220{\verb|\|}\)), except when expressed with an escape sequence started by a backslash.

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll@{\qquad\qquad}l} \def\mathdef2141#1{{}}\mathdef2141{string} & \href{../text/values.html#text-string}{\mathtt{string}} &::=& \def\mathdef2221#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2221{"}~(b{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-string}{\mathtt{stringchar}})^\ast~\def\mathdef2222#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2222{"} \quad\Rightarrow\quad b^\ast \\ \def\mathdef2141#1{{}}\mathdef2141{string character} & \href{../text/values.html#text-string}{\mathtt{stringchar}} &::=& \def\mathdef2223#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2223{~~} ~~\Rightarrow~~ \def\mathdef2224#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef2224{20} ~~~|~~~ \def\mathdef2225#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2225{!} ~~\Rightarrow~~ \def\mathdef2226#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef2226{21} \\ &&|& \def\mathdef2227#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2227{\#} ~~\Rightarrow~~ \def\mathdef2228#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef2228{23} ~~~|~~~ \cdots ~~~|~~~ \def\mathdef2229#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2229{[} ~~\Rightarrow~~ \def\mathdef2230#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef2230{5B} \\ &&|& \def\mathdef2231#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2231{]} ~~\Rightarrow~~ \def\mathdef2232#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef2232{5D} ~~~|~~~ \cdots ~~~|~~~ \def\mathdef2233#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2233{\verb|~|} ~~\Rightarrow~~ \def\mathdef2234#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef2234{7E} \\ &&|& \def\mathdef2235#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2235{\verb|\t|} ~~\Rightarrow~~ \def\mathdef2236#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef2236{09} \\ &&|& \def\mathdef2237#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2237{\verb|\n|} ~~\Rightarrow~~ \def\mathdef2238#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef2238{0A} \\ &&|& \def\mathdef2239#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2239{\verb|\r|} ~~\Rightarrow~~ \def\mathdef2240#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef2240{0D} \\ &&|& \def\mathdef2241#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2241{\verb|\"|} ~~\Rightarrow~~ \def\mathdef2242#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef2242{22} \\ &&|& \def\mathdef2243#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2243{\verb|\'|} ~~\Rightarrow~~ \def\mathdef2244#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef2244{27} \\ &&|& \def\mathdef2245#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2245{\verb|\\|} ~~\Rightarrow~~ \def\mathdef2246#1{\mathtt{0x#1}}\mathdef2246{5C} \\ &&|& \def\mathdef2247#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2247{\verb|\|}~n{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-hexdigit}{\mathtt{hexdigit}}~m{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-hexdigit}{\mathtt{hexdigit}} ~~\Rightarrow~~ 16\cdot n+m \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

Names¶

Names are strings denoting a byte sequence that must form a valid Unicode UTF-8 encoding.

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll@{\qquad}l} \def\mathdef2141#1{{}}\mathdef2141{name} & \href{../text/values.html#text-name}{\mathtt{name}} &::=& b^\ast{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-string}{\mathtt{string}} &\Rightarrow& \mathit{uc}^n & (\utf8(\mathit{uc}^n) = b^\ast \wedge n < 2^{32}) \\ \end{array}\end{split}\]

Identifiers¶

Indices can be given in both numeric and symbolic form. Symbolic identifiers standing for indices start with \(\def\mathdef2248#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2248{$}\), followed by any sequence of printable ASCII characters that does not contain a space, quotation mark, comma, semicolon, or bracket (parentheses, square brackets, or braces).

\[\begin{split}\begin{array}{llclll@{\qquad}l} \def\mathdef2141#1{{}}\mathdef2141{identifier} & \href{../text/values.html#text-id}{\mathtt{id}} &::=& \def\mathdef2249#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2249{$}~(b{:}\href{../text/values.html#text-id}{\mathtt{idchar}})^+ \\ \def\mathdef2141#1{{}}\mathdef2141{identifier character} & \href{../text/values.html#text-id}{\mathtt{idchar}} &::=& \def\mathdef2250#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2250{0} ~~|~~ \dots ~~|~~ \def\mathdef2253#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2253{Z} \\ &&|& \def\mathdef2254#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2254{a} ~~|~~ \dots ~~|~~ \def\mathdef2255#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2255{z} \\ &&|& \def\mathdef2256#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2256{!} ~~|~~ \def\mathdef2257#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2257{\#} ~~|~~ \def\mathdef2258#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2258{\$} ~~|~~ \def\mathdef2261#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2261{\verb|'|} ~~|~~ \def\mathdef2262#1{\mbox{‘}\mathtt{#1}\mbox{’}}\mathdef2262{*} ~~|~~
